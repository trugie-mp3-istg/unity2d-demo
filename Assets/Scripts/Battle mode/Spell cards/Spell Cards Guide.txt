Spell Cards are categorized into Active and Passive Spell Cards.

= Active: 
	These Spell Cards are positioned in the bottom center of the HUD. 
	They are essentially guns and weapons the player can actively select and use against enemies.
	Active Spell Cards in the deck are randomly drawn in batch into hand at the start of combat phase, and individually every few seconds,
provided there is still empty space in hand.

= Passive:
	These Spell Cards are positioned in the top left of the HUD, below the HP bar.
	They serve as, like the name suggests, passive effects that require conditions to automatically activate.

Spell Cards List:
	- 1 ~ "Snowball":
		* Active
		* Element: Water
		* Shoots a slow, huge, bullet-destroying orb at the nearest enemy, exploding and dealing 1 HP damage to all enemies in a moderate 
AoE on contact, clearing all bullets caught in it.
		(*) Elemental Overload: The orb becomes larger, lasts longer, and moves slower.

	- 2 ~ "Snow Angel":
		* Active
		* Element: Wood
		* Heals 0.25 HP.
		(*) Elemental Overload: Heals 0.5 HP instead.

	- 3 ~ "Icicle Machine Gun":
		* Passive
		* Element: Water
		* Perpetually shoots 8 fast shards per sec at the nearest enemy within a moderate range, each dealing 0.04 HP damage.

	- 4 ~ "Overpowering Fragrance":
		* Active
		* Element: Wood
		* Shoots fast bullets in 8 directions 3 times, each dealing 0.5 HP damage.
		(*) Elemental Overload: Bullets deal more damage and gain Bouncing.

	- 5 ~ "Break of Dawn":
		* Active
		* Element: Fire
		* Attacks in a fixed horizontal line across the nearest enemy in view, dealing 3 HP to all enemies caught.
		(*) Elemental Overload: Adds an additional vertical line across said enemy.
